Welcome toProtoHISTORYbowl

ProtoHISTORYbowl is a realtime, single-player National History Bowl and Bee resource. A single-player session can include one person with instantaneous buzzing. It can be used in single-player mode online or offline and includes over 400 questions.


Enter the name of a room to go to or simply hit the go button to create a new empty room. You can't invite friends to join you by sharing the URL.


If you're feeling particularly brave right now, you can venture into a private room such as B-Set Bowl , C-Set Bowl , C-Set Bee , or Third Quarter .

We're also working on specialty/niche rooms such as Countries , States , Cities , Assassinations , Strikes , European Monarchs , Russia , Africa , Wars , Battles , Asian , Ancient , Art , and Music .
