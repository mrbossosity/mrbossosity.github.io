Welcome toProtoHISTORYbowl

ProtoHISTORYbowl is a realtime, single-player National History Bowl and Bee resource. A single-player session can include one person with instantaneous buzzing. It can be used in single-player mode online or offline and includes over 200 questions.


Enter the name of a room to go to or simply hit the go button to create a new empty room. You can't invite friends to join you by sharing the URL.


If you're feeling particularly brave right now, you can venture into a private room such as A-Set , B-Set , or C-Set .
